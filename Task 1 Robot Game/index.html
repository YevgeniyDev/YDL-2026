Mission & scoring

Grab your key + coins, then both reach your door. acid kills both. R restarts a level.

Stand on a button to hold its gate open for your partner.

Stomp enemies from above for points — side contact is fatal.

Push crates into place or onto buttons; climb them to reach ledges.

Score = coins + clear bonus + speed bonus − deaths.
