# Application periods and deadlines

Important: the foundation's programs run in annual cycles. Below are the typical periods and the dates of the latest known cycle. Exact dates for the next year are usually published on the official site in January–February of that year.

## Shakhmardan Yessenov Scholarship
- Cycle: annual.
- Applications: usually early February to early March.
- 2026 cycle: applications were accepted Feb 2 – March 3, 2026 (until 3:00 PM, GMT+5).
- Results: 20 winners announced in the first week of April.
- Dates for 2027 and beyond will be published on the foundation site in January–February.

## Yessenov Data Lab (YDL)
- Cycle: annual summer school.
- Applications: in spring. For the 2026 cycle, applications were accepted until April 28, 2026.
- The program itself (a ~4-week intensive) usually runs in June–July.
- Next year's dates are published on the foundation site in advance.

## Research Internships
- Cycle: annual. Applications open early in the year; exact dates are in the Program Provisions on the site.

## Yessenov Launch Pad
- Cycle: annual grants for young professionals' skills development; dates on the foundation site.

If applications for the program you're interested in have already closed this year, watch yessenovfoundation.org or leave your contact — a manager will let you know when a new cycle opens.
