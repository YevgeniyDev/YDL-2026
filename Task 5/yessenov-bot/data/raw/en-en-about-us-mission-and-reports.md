Galimzhan Yessenov founded the Academic Shakhmardan Yessenov Science and Education Foundation in 2013. The Foundation’s objective is to support students and young Kazakhstani scientists who specialize in the exact and natural sciences to continue main education, to improve their professionalism and competitiveness. THE FOUNDATION’S MISSION: to develop Kazakhstan’s intellectual potential.
