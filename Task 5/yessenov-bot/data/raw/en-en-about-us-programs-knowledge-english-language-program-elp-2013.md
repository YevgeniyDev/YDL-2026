In 2013-2014 , the program was held for the first time at the KSUTI IM University. Sh. Esenova in the city of Aktau. During the year, 60 students and teachers from the Faculty of Oil and Gas, and Transport and Construction were trained free of charge from the teachers of the prestigious international school Bonas MacFarlane.

At the end of the program, the participants shared their feedback on the program.

Feoktistova Elena, Associate Professor of the Department of Foreign Languages, Head of the Department of Academic Mobility

I would like to thank you very much for having organized such courses for our teachers with the participation of Mark. In my opinion, they are very useful and productive. I now constantly use the acquired knowledge and experience in practice. Recently I was invited to the regional PKI to give lectures for school teachers, it was very useful.

Bisembaeva Aigerim, 1st year student, Faculty of Oil and Gas

Tom’s classes are very interesting. Learning English directly with a native speaker is more effective and rewarding. I learn new rules that other teachers have not explained before. Communication with Tom helps us develop oral communication skills, listening comprehension, also helps us overcome psychological and language barriers.
