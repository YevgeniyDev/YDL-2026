title: The English Language Program 2015-2016 — Shakhmardan Yessenov Foundation
---

In the 2015-2016 the program was held at the KSUTI University named after Shakhmardan Yessenov in the city of Aktau. During the year, 56 students and teachers from the Faculty of Oil and Gas, and Transport and Construction were trained free of charge from the teachers of the prestigious international school Bonas MacFarlane. At the end of the program, the participant who demonstrated the greatest progress in training received the opportunity to attend an intensive English language course in the UK at the expense of the Yesenov Foundation.
