This year the program trains 120 students and teachers of Kyzylorda State University. The program lasts 9 months, after which students will receive international certificates

Shakhmardan Yessenov Science and Education Foundation announces the competition for English courses by one of Great Britain’s leading language schools, Bonas Macfarlane Education. Highly qualified teachers from the UK will conduct the courses.

Only teachers of KSU Korkyt Ata and students of the following faculties can participate in this competition:

•School of «Biology, geography and chemistry» of Natural sciences faculty,

•Engineering and technology faculty,

•Engineering and ecology faculty,

•Faculty of economics and information technologies

If you have leadership qualities, If you are thinking about a successful career in the future and if you plan to continue your education abroad, don’t miss the chance to submit your application!

Applications will be accepted till 10 May, 2017 (23:59).
