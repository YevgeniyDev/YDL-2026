The second year of the program of studying English in Kyzylorda State University starts 1 October . Competition based program gives an opportunity to 120 students and teachers to study English throughout the year. The program lasts 9 months , by the end of it all participants get international level certificates and the best students and teachers get a chance to pass official IELTS certification for free .

Courses are taught by one of Great Britain’s leading language schools Bonas Macfarlane Education. Competition is open to teachers and 2,3,4 year students of Kyzylorda State University . In total, the program provides 120 grants, 30 grants allocated to teachers of multilingual faculties, 50 grants to the students of multilingual faculties and 40 grants to students regardless of their faculties.

If you study at Kyzylorda State University, dream of having better English, and think about your future prospects then we are waiting for your application package.

Applications should be brought to the office of International Office from 17 till 21 September , 2018.
