title: English Language Program 2019-2021 — Shakhmardan Yessenov Foundation

Shakhmardan Yessenov Foundation is launching a competition among regional universities of Kazakhstan, that awards a grant to the university to conduct a 2-year English language program.

Only regional universities of Kazakhstan located outside the cities of Astana and Almaty participate in the competition.

The annual number of grants is 160 , of which 90 are for undergraduate students, 45 for teachers, and 25 for masters and PhD students. Classes run twice a week (each class lasts 90 minutes) for 8 months from October 2019 to May 2021. At the end of the second year, 20 teachers and 15 students with the best attendance, engagement (homework) and progress test results (initial, intermediate and final) are given the opportunity to take an IELTS test at the expense of the Foundation.

Application are to be sent in digital format to info@yessenovfoundation.org . Applications are accepted until 31 March , 2019. The winner will be announced on 30 April, 2019.
