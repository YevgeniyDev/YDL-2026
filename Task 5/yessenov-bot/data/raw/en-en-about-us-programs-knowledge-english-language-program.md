The English Language Program is conducted together with Bonas MacFarlane International School among regional higher educational institutions of Kazakhstan, in order to help students across the country improve and validate their English language abilities. The university winning the foundation competition will conduct the program on its premises for a period of 2 years . As per the foundation grant, 2 certified lecturers, who are native English speakers, will be sent to the university to teach both students and teachers. Upon completion of the program, the best 50 teachers and students will get a chance to participate in the IELTS test free of charge at the expense of the Foundation – an increase from 35 per year in the years until 2022. Since 2020, the program has been implemented online.
