The program was created to inform high school students about new opportunities for professional and personal growth that have emerged in Kazakhstan and around the world. It features a series of lectures led by experts and professionals in their respective fields, covering topics that are highly relevant to graduating students — choosing a career path and university, defining one’s life trajectory, writing a compelling motivation letter, self-presentation and interview skills, fact-checking and critical thinking, applying to various programs, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and more. Since its launch in 2018 , the program has reached over 1,000 participants, including students and their parents, offering them valuable insights and practical guidance for shaping their future.
