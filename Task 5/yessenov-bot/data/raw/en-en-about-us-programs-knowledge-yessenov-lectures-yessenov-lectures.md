The program of guest lectures, “Yessenov lectures” is a course of lectures from well-known professionals in various fields, hosted by the Foundation in the best Kazakhstani universities. Guest lectures by company representatives allow students to gain a better understanding of the requirements of the labor market, as well as to realize their capabilities and evaluate their potential.

All lectures can be viewed on our YouTube channel.
