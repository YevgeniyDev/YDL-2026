A summer school for current data analysts and those planning to work in this field. The program was initiated in 2018 and is an intensive course lasting several weeks (in recent years – online), designed to train young scientists and specialists from Kazakhstan in the fundamental skills of big data analysis for application in their future work.

- Yessenov Data Lab 2026 20 grants for young scientists to the summer school for data analysis. Yessenov Data Lab 2026
