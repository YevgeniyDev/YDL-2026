The program provides an opportunity for ten best students who have completed two competitive selections to receive sponsorship for a summer internship in laboratories around the world in 2016

Terms of the program:

Eligibility: students of 2,3 courses of Kazakh universities that study in science, technology or specialties related to Nutrition / Food Studies and Public Health.

Number of grants: 10

Required documents:

- a copy of the identity card;

- a copy of the record book (all sessions);

- A copy of the certificate of secondary education and the insert;

- copies of diplomas, certificates, competitions of scientific works;

- an essay on the topic “I need a PhD degree for …”;

- recommendation of the pro-rector or rector of the university;

Deadline for submission of documents: until 15.12.2015

Notes: The application with all the package of documents must be sent to the e-mail address of the Foundation: info@vessenovfoundation.org.
