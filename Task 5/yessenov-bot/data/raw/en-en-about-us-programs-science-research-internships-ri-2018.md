title: Research Internships in Laboratories for Graduate Students and Specialists 2018 — Shakhmardan Yessenov Foundation
---

Provision of 5 grants to the graduate students and/or specialists with major in natural sciences to undertake a research internship in laboratories around the world with the subsequent opportunity of applying to a Ph.D program in the future (undertaken by the students themselves).

Program eligibility

The Program is open to (a) working research specialists with a degree no higher than Master’s, (b) undergraduate students in their senior year and (c) graduate students (both 1 st and 2 nd year) majoring in engineering and natural sciences and enrolled in universities of Kazakhstan. Applicants must be citizens of the Republic of Kazakhstan .

Number of grants : 5

Grant amount: 7000 US Dollars.

Required documents :

- CV;

- Scan copy of applicant’s national ID;

- Scan copy of applicant’s official transcript and bachelor degree diploma (for graduate students and working specialists);

- Copies of diplomas, certificates, scientific projects, etc.

- An essay on the subject: “I would like pursue to science because…”

- Recommendation letter(s) from research advisor

Application call is open from Jan 8 through Feb 18, 2018

Application and all the required documents must be sent to the Foundation’s e-mail: info@yessenovfoundation.org .
