One of the main requirements for admission to postgraduate and doctoral studies of leading universities in the world is experience in research laboratories. Because of the lack of opportunity to undertake the necessary internships, many Kazakh students do not receive this experience and are therefore deprived of the prospects for admission to the world’s top universities to continue their scientific studies. To address this lack of access and help talented students secure the experience they need, this program was first introduced in 2013 .
