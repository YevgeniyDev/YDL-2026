Participation of the scientists in scientific conferences of international level enables young scientists to present the work of Kazakhstanis to the whole world, develop professional skills; allows to expand the range of knowledge and gives impetus to the development of a particular scientific field, as well as provides an opportunity to exchange experience with foreign colleagues. The development of this program was one of the activities of the Foundation for the formation of domestic scientific personnel.

The program of sponsorship of the scientific-educational fund. academician Shahmardan Yesenov, scientists, students, teachers for participation in international scientific conferences was aimed at supporting talented Kazakhstanis engaged in scientific activity.

One of the most popular programs of the fund, operating from the beginning of the fund in 2013 to 2017. More than 6 000 000 tenge was allocated for the entire period of implementation of the program on travel grants.
