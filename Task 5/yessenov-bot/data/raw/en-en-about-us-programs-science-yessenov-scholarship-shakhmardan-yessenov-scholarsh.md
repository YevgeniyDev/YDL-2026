The Scientific and Educational Foundation. Academician Shahmardan Yesenova announces a competition for the provision of five * nominal monthly student scholarships. The term of the grant: 12 months (from April 2016 to March 2017).

The terms of the program:

Who can participate: citizens of the Republic of Kazakhstan who study at Kazakh universities on 2-3 courses of bachelor’s degree or 1 year of magistracy

Number of grants: 5

Required documents:

- application form (available on the website of the fund yessenovfoundation.org);

- recommendation of the scientific adviser (the head of the bachelor’s program / master’s program, the head of the department);

- motivational appeal: “Convince us to invite you to the third stage of the competition” (any form of appeal: audio, video, written);

- A copy of the identity card;

- transcript (the average score should not be lower than 3);

- A copy of the diploma of higher education for students of a master’s degree (bachelor, specialist).

Deadline for submission of documents: until 10.03.2016.

Notes: The competition consists of three rounds. Until mid-April 2016 a list of five holders of nominal scholarships will be published to them. academician Shahmardan Yesenov.
