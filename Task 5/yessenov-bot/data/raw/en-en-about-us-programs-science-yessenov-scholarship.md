The program is designed to promote the development of technical and natural science disciplines in Kazakhstan by providing financial support to the best undergraduate students and developing their competencies in demand by modern society. The program has been in existence since 2013 . Having started as a scholarship program exclusively for students of Satbayev University, today the Yessenov Scholarship covers students of more than 30 higher educational institutions of the country.
